Data Deletion Request for MusicTube App

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. If you wish to delete your data associated with the MusicTube app, we provide a simple and straightforward process to handle your request.

How to Request Data Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to our designated contact address below
Step 2: Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request - MusicTube"
Step 3: Include any relevant information that can help us identify your data (such as device ID if known)
Step 4: We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email

Email Template:

To: musictubeapp.contact@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - MusicTube

Message: "I would like to request the deletion of all my data associated with the MusicTube app. Please confirm once the deletion is complete."

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will remove the following data associated with your use of the MusicTube app:

  • App Analytics Data: Information about how you interact with the app
  • Crash Reports: Technical logs collected when the app encounters errors
  • Diagnostic Information: Performance and usage data for app improvement
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous device IDs and advertising IDs collected through Firebase and AdMob

Important Note: The MusicTube app does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, real email address, or other personal details. The data we collect is primarily anonymous and used for app analytics, crash reporting, and advertising purposes.

Processing Timeline

We are committed to processing your data deletion request promptly:

  • Acknowledgment of your request: Within 3 business days
  • Complete data deletion: Within 30 days
  • Confirmation email: Sent upon completion of the deletion process
